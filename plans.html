Story Points
  • Dreamers are people born with prophetic dreams who awaken during puberty.
  • Dreamers can live for hundred of years. Slowly losing their blessing over time.
  • Dreamers sometimes see the future, but more often see glimpses of the past.
  • The last dreamer was born over 120 years ago.
  • There are only 7 known to be currently alive. Six of them make up The Council.
  • The 7th dreamer left the The Council after having a dream of his own death.
  • No one has seen the 7th in 70 years.
  • You start your journey as one of the 4 starter disciplines
  • Game Opens with you having a dream about The Council of 7 having an argument.
  • Through story you find out you are a Dreamer
Game Mechanics - Disciplines
The starting Disciplines when creating a character are:
  • Cadet (Melee)
  • Scout (Ranged)
  • Acolyte (Healing Magic)
  • Apprentice (Damage Magic)
After leveling up and spending points on the skills tree. You can unlock an Advanced Discpline:
  • Cadet > Warrior, Knight, or Brawler
  • Scout > Trapper, Ranger, or Handler
  • Acolyte > Cleric, Druid, or Shaman
  • Apprentice> Wizard, Elementalist, or Warlock
You will be able to master a discipline by spending enough points in the tree. Once you have master 2 disciplines you unlock Mastery Discuplines. These futher modify skills, adding passives, or other decided changes.
See Actions below under Tier 2 Disciplines

Game Mechanics - Actions

***Undergoing Changes Regularly***


Actions fall into 4 categories:

  1. Attack (Atk)
  2. Buff (Bf)
  3. Combat Action (CA)
  4. Passive (Psv)

You assign Attacks and Combat Buff's to your main 4 slots. Buffs/Aura go on your secondary/buff slots. Using the controller (xbox) the ABXY will hold your 4 main abilities you wish to have active, holding Right Trigger (R2) will change the ABXY onscreen menu to the buff bar/cross putting buff/aura abilities on ABXY. This allows players to buff up/turn on off auras quickly without having to us up one of their 4 action slots. Combat Actions are skills that alter your character while in combat that you would want to utilized a ABYX key for but do not deal damage to the enemy. Example of this are Panic and Play Dead.

Passive abilities are active at all times as long as the Discipline is currently set in your Primary or secondary Discipline.


Starter Discipline


  • Cadet
    • Charge (Atk) - Move toward enemy quickly and deal damage on impact.
      1. Going The Distance - Increases Range
      2. Move It - Paths Through Enemies In The Way
      3. Sweep The Leg - Apply Slow Debuff
      4. Impale - Applies bleed to target
    • Rally (bf) - Bonus Damage (Buff) - 2 Minutes
      1. Double Time - Buff Lasts 5 Minute
      2. Battle Standard - Double Damage Bonus
      3. Intimidation - Monsters around you while rally is active deal 10% less damage
      4. No Down Time - Buff reset every time you user Charge.
    • Panic (CA) - Minus Damage Dealt, Minus Damage Taken, Increased Movement Speed (2 Seconds)
      1. Self Control - Damage Dealt Penalty Removed
      2. Well Guarded - Damage Reduction Bonus Doubled
      3. Retreat - Movement Speed Bonus Increased to 5 Seconds
      4. Get Back - Knockback Enemies when ability used.
    • Strike (Atk) - Swings weapon in front of you hitting up to 3 enemies in a small melee ranged cone.
      1. Wild Strike - Increases number of targets to 5 and makes cone wider
      2. Triple Strike - On the third consecutive Strike attack, critical strike chance is doubled.
      3. Barbed Strike - Strike has a 20% chance to cause bleeding.
      4. Tactical Strike - Strike has a 25% chance of returning energy cost.

  • Scout

    • Take Aim (Atk)- High Damage Ranged Skill (Bow, Thrown, Etc) 1.5 Second Cast Time
      1. Quick Draw - Decreases cast time by .75 Seconds
      2. Serrated - 10% Chance to cause bleeding on hit.
      3. True Target - Once a target has been hit with Aim, All consecutives Take Aim or Piercing Projectiles will seek that target. Using any ability other than Take Aim or Piercing Projectiles resets True Target
      4. Pinning - 15% Chance to bind enemy for 2 seconds
    • Cheer (Bf) - Regen 2 HP/ 1 AP per tick (Buff) - 1 Minute
      1. Sing It - Increases buff time to 5 minutes
      2. Pitch Perfect - HP/AP increased to 5/2
      3. Project - Cheer now effects party members within 15 meters
      4. Chorus - Cheer timer resets whenever you use a potion
    • Strafe (CA) - Increased Movement Speed (5 Seconds) - 1 second cooldown
      1. Quick Twitch - Increases movement bonus by 20%
      2. Endurance Training - Increases strafe to 10 seconds
      3. Acrobatics - Jump backward 5 meters when strafe is used.
      4. Cunning - Strafe also gives you a 20% chance to avoid being hit.
    • Piercing Projectile - Ranged skill that pierces enemies dealing 25% less damage per enemy hit.
      1. Barbed - Reduces damage by 33% for each target, has 25% chance to cause bleed.
      2. Sharpened Edge - Projectiles now do 20% less damage per target
      3. Heavy - Projectiles have a 10% chance to knockback on hit
      4. Efficient Projectiles - Every target hit by your projectile restores 1 AP.

  • Acolyte

    • Holy Spark (Atk) - Magic Damage that flows from the player randomly toward enemies (Similar to spark in Diablo)
    • Vigor (Bf) - Health Point Regen Buff
    • Heal (CA) - Restore Hit Points

  • Apprentice
    • Energy Bolt - Single target magic damage
    • Concentrate - Action Point Regen Buff
    • Blink (CA) - Short distance teleport in direction player is facing. Walls and Objects prevent blinking.

Tier 1 Disciplines - Cadet


  • Warrior - Requires Dual Wield or Two-Handed Weapon
    • Charge > Intercept - Inherits all charge upgrades while adding an AOE splash damage around the final target.
    • Rally > Shout - Inherits all Rally upgrades and also applies an AOE Debuff increasing damage taken by 15%
    • Panic > Enrage - Inherits all Panic upgrades but Increased Damage Dealt by 50% and Damage Taken by 25%
    • Strike > Slam - Inherits all Strike upgrades and increases damage dealt by 25% and has a 5% chance to confuse for 3 seconds
  • Knight - Requires One-Handed Weapon and Shield/Guard
    • Charge > Shield Slam - Inherits all charge upgrades, but you charge with your shield, preventing spell casting for 5 seconds. Replaces Impale with Knock Senseless.
      • Knock Senseless - Reduces damage dealt by 20% for 10 seconds.
    • Rally > Inspire - Rally now affects party members within 30 meters. Reduces damage taken by 20%
    • Panic > Combat Orders - Inherits all panic upgrades and causes a knockback when movement speed ends. Combat Orders speed boost effects everyone in the party.
    • Strike > Spinning Strike - Player now spins around hitting enemies in a 360 degree arc. Target limit removed.
  • Brawler - Requires Fist/Martial Weapon
    • Charge > Bull Rush - Inherits all charge upgrades and is a guaranteed critical hit.
    • Rally > Instigate - Inherits all Rally upgrades and causes enemies around you to come into melee range.
    • Panic > Adrenaline - Inherits all Panic upgrades and increases attack speed by 20%
    • Strike > Pummel - Inherits all Strike upgrades and causes strike to happen 3 times in a row with 200% increased attack speed.

Scout


  • Ranger - Requires Ranged Weapon
    • Take Aim > True Target - Inherits all Take Aim upgrades, targeting an enemy True Target will lock your projectiles onto that enemy for every follow up True Target attack. Any other ability resets True Target.
    • Cheer > XXXXX - Inherits all Cheer upgrades, also awards 2 additional AP regeneration.
    • Strafe > XXXXX - Inherits all Strafe upgrades, removes AP cost but adds 5 second cool down.
    • Piercing Projectile > Deadly Projectiles - Inherits all Piercing Projectile upgrades and increases critical strike chance of Deadly Projectiles by 25%
  • Hunter - Requires Ranged Weapon
    • Summon Companion (CA) - Summons you beast companion
    • Cheer > Soothing - Cheer now has a chance to tame a beast within range, if the hunter does not currently have a companion.
    • Strafe > XXXXX - Inherits all Strafe upgrades, strafe also applies to your beast companion.
    • Piercing Projectiles > XXXXX - Inherits all Piercing Projectile upgrades and causes companion to dash to nearest enemy hit by projectile.
  • Musician - Requires Musical Instrument
    • Take Aim > Riff - Inherits all Take Aim upgrades, removes cast time and turns ability into channeled skill. Damages target every tick.
    • Cheer > Crescendo - Inherits all Cheer upgrades, becomes channeled buff that increases HP/AP regen to
    • Strafe > Affrettando - Inherits all strafe upgrades and causes the the player to increase movement speed over time until speed is 200%
    • Piercing Projectiles > Cacophony - Inherits all Piercing Projectiles upgrades and causes targets hit by Cacophony to become silenced for 2 seconds
  • Wizard
    • Fireball (Atk) - Fire Based Damage, explodes on contact dealing fire splash damage
    • Gust (Atk) - Aoe Cone Wind Based Damage
    • Stalactite (Atk) - Aoe spot picked on map that deals Earth Based Damage
    • Ice Spear (Atk) - Straight line piecing Water Based Damage that has a chance to freeze on hit. Stops when freeze occurs.
  • Cleric
    • Divine Light (Atk/CA)- Holy spell that deals Holy Damage to unfriendly target or Heals a friendly target.
    • Resurrect (CA) - Bring dead player/npc/pet/summon back to life
    • Regen (Bf)- HP Regen Buff

Tier 2 Disciplines


  • Mercenary (Warrior + Ranger)

    • Tactics (Psv) - Increased Defense/Damage Reduction
  • War Mage (Warrior + Wizard)
    • Enchant Weapon (Psv) - Elemental Magic (Pick 1) Applied to Weapon (+Elemental Damage)
    • Enchant Armor (Psv) - Elemental Magic (Pick 1) Applied To Armor (+Def Element)
  • Champion (Warrior + Cleric)
    • Holy Shroud (Psv) - % Magic Damage Reduction
    • Inspire (Bf) - Stat Boost Aura
  • Spell Thief (Ranger + Wizard)
    • Drain Mana (Bf) - Applies mana steal to melee\ranged weapon
    • Interrupt (CA) - Ranged spell that interrupts and Prevents Spell Cast for 5 seconds.
  • Artificer (Ranger + Cleric)
    • Healing Brew (CA) - Healing Spell + Short Buff
    • Damage Brew (Atk) - Damage + Debuff
    • Blessed Balistics (Bf) - Holy Damage Ranged Weapon Buff
  • Zealot (Cleric + Wizard)
    • Fear (CA) - Debuff
    • Holy Shield (Psv) - Magic & Physical Damage Reduction
